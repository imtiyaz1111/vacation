4 Days Delhi Jaipur Ranthambore Tour Itinerary

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival Delhi – Jaipur

Morning arrival in Delhi by your driver. Pickup from airport or railway station and drive to Jaipur (approx. 6 hours).

Upon arrival in Jaipur check-in to hotel. Later visit City Palace and Jantar Mantar. Evening free for local market shopping. Overnight stay at Jaipur.

Day 02: Jaipur Sightseeing Tour

After breakfast proceed for Jaipur sightseeing. Visit Amber Fort, Jal Mahal, Hawa Mahal and Jantar Mantar Observatory.

Later drive to Ranthambore. On arrival check-in to hotel. Overnight stay at Ranthambore.

Day 03: Ranthambore Sightseeing

After breakfast enjoy morning jeep/canter safari in Ranthambore National Park. Spot wildlife including tigers, leopards, deer and birds.

Visit Ranthambore Fort located inside the reserve. Return to hotel and overnight stay at Ranthambore.

Day 04: Ranthambore – Delhi

After breakfast check out from hotel and drive back to Delhi (approx. 8 hours).

On arrival transfer to airport or railway station for onward journey.
