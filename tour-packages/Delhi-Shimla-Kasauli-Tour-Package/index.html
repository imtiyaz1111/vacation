2 Days Delhi Shimla Kasauli Tour Itinerary

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 01: Delhi City Tour

You can explore both Old and New Delhi in this full-day tour with a private local guide. The tour moves comfortably between the historical monuments of Delhi in a private air-conditioned vehicle. Pick up from Airport or Hotel in Delhi.

The tour would start by meeting the tour driver cum guide with whom the client would explore the city in and out. The tour would proceed to magnificent monuments of historic importance: Humayun Tomb, Qutub Minar, Raj Ghat, President House (only delegates are allowed), India Gate, Parliament Street, Lotus Temple, Red Fort (Outside View), Jama Masjid.

After Delhi sightseeing, return to the hotel and spend the evening at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 02: Delhi – Shimla Kasauli – Delhi (Return Trip)

Early morning pickup from hotel and proceed to Shimla Kasauli. After arrival in Shimla Kasauli, visit Taj Mahal (Symbol of Love), Shimla Kasauli Fort, and Baby Taj Mahal (Itmad-ud-Daula).

After sightseeing, return back to Delhi and drop at hotel or airport.

Note: Taj Mahal remains closed on Friday.
