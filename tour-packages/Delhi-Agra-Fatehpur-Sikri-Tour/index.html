2 Days Delhi Agra Fatehpur Sikri Tour Itinerary

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 01: Delhi – Agra

Pickup from your hotel and drive to Agra via Yamuna Expressway (approx. 3 hours). On arrival, check-in to your hotel and after lunch begin sightseeing.

Visit Taj Mahal, Agra Fort and Mehtab Bagh. Enjoy sunrise/sunset view of Taj Mahal and explore the Mughal architecture and beautiful gardens.

Later enjoy leisure time and stay overnight in hotel.

Day 02: Agra – Fatehpur Sikri – Delhi

After breakfast depart for Fatehpur Sikri (approx. 40 kms from Agra). Visit the historical abandoned city and explore its monuments with guide.

After sightseeing, drive back to Delhi and drop at hotel or airport for onward journey.
