2 Days Delhi Jaipur Tour Itinerary

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 01: Delhi – Jaipur

Pick up from Delhi by your English speaking driver and straight drive to Jaipur (approx. 250 kms / 5-6 hrs). On the way stop over break with washroom and snacks.

On arrival check-in to the hotel in Jaipur and after relaxation visit City Palace and Jantar Mantar observatory.

Evening will be free for leisure activities and shopping in the local bazaars. Overnight stay at hotel in Jaipur.

Day 02: Jaipur Sightseeing Tour – Delhi Drop

After breakfast check out from the hotel and proceed for city sightseeing tour of Jaipur. Visit Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Wind, a UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its Rajput architecture.

Explore Jal Mahal while driving towards Amber Fort. Visit the magnificent Amber Fort, built with white marble and red sandstone, known for its beautiful architecture and history.

Elephant ride at Amber Fort is an enjoyable experience where beautifully decorated elephants are available for a royal ride at an economical price.

After completing sightseeing, drive back to Delhi and drop you at hotel or airport.
