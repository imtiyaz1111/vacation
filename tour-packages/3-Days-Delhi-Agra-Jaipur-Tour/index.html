3 Days Delhi Agra Jaipur Tour Itinerary

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 01: New Delhi – Agra

Start your trip with a pickup from the airport / railway station / hotel or other desired place in Delhi. After that, you will proceed for a full-day city tour of Delhi visiting the 11th century The Qutub Minar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site built by Qutub-ud-din Aibek of the Slave Dynasty.

Visit Humayun's Tomb, the Bahai Temple (also known as Lotus Temple, closed on Mondays), India Gate, Parliament House, President’s Palace and Secretariat buildings.

After sightseeing, enjoy shopping in New Delhi. Later you will be taken to Agra via a 3-hour drive on the expressway. Upon arrival in Agra, check-in to your hotel and relax.

Day 02: Agra – Jaipur

At sunrise, visit the world-famous monument of love – The Taj Mahal (closed on Fridays). After breakfast, visit Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

After that, enjoy some free time for shopping in Agra. Then, have lunch at a local restaurant (own expense) and transfer to the Pink City of Jaipur.

Upon arrival in Jaipur, check-in to your hotel and relax.

Day 03: Jaipur – Delhi

After breakfast, proceed for sightseeing tour of Jaipur city. Visit Amber Fort, situated on top of Amber Hill. Later, stop for photos at Jal Mahal (Lake Palace) and Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds).

Visit City Palace, the royal residence of Jaipur, and Jantar Mantar Observatory, an astronomical observation site and UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Enjoy lunch in a local restaurant (own expense) and free time for shopping in Jaipur. Later in the afternoon, drive back to Delhi and get dropped at your hotel, airport, railway station, or any desired location.
